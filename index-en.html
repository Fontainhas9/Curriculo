Master's Thesis

December 2022

Carbon Footprint Calculator in partnership with Hydra IT

Braga, Portugal

As part of the Master's dissertation in Information Systems Engineering and Management, I developed a module in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central with the goal of calculating the carbon footprint associated with products. The work arose from the growing need for companies to evaluate and report the environmental impact of their operations, in line with international best sustainability practices.

The project was supported by recognized methodologies, such as the GHG Protocol and Life Cycle Assessment (LCA), which ensured consistency and scientific rigor in the calculations. Based on these foundations, I defined the functional and technical requirements necessary for the system to be modular, generic, and adaptable to different industrial contexts, minimizing changes whenever it was applied in new organizations.

The implementation was carried out in AL language, leveraging tables, pages, and ERP flows to create an integrated and practical solution. The developed functionalities include the calculation of direct and indirect emissions associated with the production process, as well as the possibility of obtaining estimates of expected emissions and actual values based on the data effectively recorded in the system. To validate the solution, practical examples of products and production lines were used, allowing the applicability of the module in real-world scenarios to be proven.

Additionally, I prepared detailed documentation and a user manual intended for end-users, ensuring a faster and more efficient adoption of the tool. Beyond the technical development, this work contributed to reinforcing business awareness of sustainability and environmental impact, showing how it is possible to combine management technology with environmental responsibility practices.

The dissertation was presented in 2022 as part of the Master's in Information Systems Engineering and Management at the University of Minho, consolidating technical skills in software development, information systems integration, and the application of environmental methodologies in a business context.
